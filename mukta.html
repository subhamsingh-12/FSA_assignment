Mukta Arts consolidated March 2020 Net sales at Rs. 40.56 crore, down 7.55% Y-o-Y

Mukta Arts Ltd. is an Indian film production company, headquartered in Mumbai, estabilished in 1978. The company is involved in production, distribution, exibition and education in cinema and creative arts and production of digital software in TV series, web series and documentaries. Subhash Ghai is the founder and chairman of Mukta Arts while Rahul Puri is the MD. Prabuddha Gupta is the CFO of the group. Mukta arts was first Indian film production company to establish the practice of securing film insurance through its movie 'TAAL'.
